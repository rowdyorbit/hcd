Who We Are

Teresa Y. Hodge

Founder and Managing Member

As a businesswoman, prison was never in Teresa's projected future. Yet, when she began serving an 87-month sentence in 2007 she received her indoctrination of mass incarceration in America. Teresa remained audacious in her belief that prison did not have the power to ruin her life, for the rest of her life. It was that belief she clinged to and has guided her to accomplish so much upon her release.

Upon returning home in 2011, she and her daughter Laurin Hodge co-founded Mission: Launch, Inc. a nonprofit focused on introducing technology and entrepreneurship to previously incarcerated individuals as a way of ensuring self-sufficiency. Additionally, the organization manages the Rebuilding Re-Entry Coalition, a citizen-led movement committed to creating a more just and inclusive society for formerly incarcerated persons. Mission: Launch operates in the Baltimore and Washington, DC region.

In 2016, Teresa stepped away from Mission: Launch’s daily activity when she was named a 2016-2017 Open Society Soros Justice Advocacy Fellow. Her project: Unlock Inclusion focuses on on advocating for digital inclusion for Americans with an arrest and/or conviction record.

Dr. Pamela Y. Keye

Chief Accountability Officer

Dr. Pamela Y. Keye is a former award winning K-12 educator and was a sought-after presenter, who used accountability systems and data to improve organizational performance and student achievement. While serving a 78-month federal prison sentence, she leveraged her expertise to develop and manage an innovative reentry office and program serving the female inmate population at FCI Coleman in Florida.

A consequence of her incarceration kept her from returning to her first love – public education. Thus, she repurposed herself as both academician and entrepreneur. Today she utilizes her expertise as a researcher, evaluator, and program designer and serves as the Chief Accountability Officer for HCD Labs, where she is designing our online and offline workforce and entrepreneurship curriculum.
